About Bee Gees

Bee Gees is a music group band that started in the 50s. They were created by the brothers of Barry, Robin, and Maurice Gibb. All three were born in a very tiny island called the Isle of Man.

The three brothers were very successful in the 1970s through the 80s until the 2 brothers passed away in the 21st century (Robin and Maurice).

The Bee Gees wrote their own disco songs and people around the world who knows them love them! Their popular song was called, "Stayin' Alive"!
